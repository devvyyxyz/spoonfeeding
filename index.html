No Spoon Feeding

Spoon-feeding helps no one. Let’s encourage learning and problem-solving instead!

Common Examples of Spoon-Feeding

  • "Can you just give me the code?"
  • "What’s the exact solution to this problem?"
  • "I don’t want to research, just tell me!"

Better Approaches

  • "Can you point me to some resources to solve this?"
  • "What’s the concept behind this problem?"
  • "I’ve tried X and Y, but I’m stuck on Z, can you help?"

Want the Answer Anyway?

Remember, learning happens when you figure things out yourself!
